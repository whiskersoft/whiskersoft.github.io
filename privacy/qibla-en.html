Data We Collect and Process

Location (approximate/precise): We use your device location to calculate the Qibla direction and, optionally, to display prayer times. Location data is primarily processed on the device. When fetching prayer times, only your latitude/longitude is transmitted anonymously to the Aladhan service. This data is not stored by us, is not linked to your identity by us, and is not used to track you in the background while the app is closed.

Device sensors (compass, accelerometer, gyroscope): These are used in real time to keep the Qibla arrow aligned and stable. Sensor data is not sent to external systems and is not logged by us.

Network access and third-party content: For the audio Quran feature, MP3 files are streamed from VerseByVerseQuran / EveryAyah (versebyversequran.com, everyayah.com). Some text/data files may be hosted on GitHub (githubusercontent.com). In these requests, your IP address and client information can be seen by the respective server as part of normal internet communication. The app itself does not implement any additional tracking or analytics.

Payments and subscriptions: Premium features, donations, or subscription purchases within the app are handled via the Google Play Billing system. Your credit card details, full payment history, and financial data are not visible to us, are not processed by us, and are not stored by us. All payment processing is performed under Google‚Äôs security and privacy standards.

Local storage / caching: Audio files and some content may be cached locally on your device to improve performance. This data is stored in the app‚Äôs local storage area. You can remove it by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Notifications: Local notifications are scheduled for prayer time reminders and other alerts. These notifications may include sound and vibration. You can disable them at any time from within the app or via your device settings.

Advertising ID and technical data (Google AdMob): For ad delivery, frequency capping, fraud and abuse prevention, and legal compliance, the advertising ID (AAID), device model, OS version, approximate location derived from IP address, and similar technical signals may be processed by Google AdMob and its advertising partners.
